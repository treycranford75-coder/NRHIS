## NRHIS Sprint 2 — Build058

Build058 adds a unified reservoir operations summary for Choke Canyon Reservoir and Lake Corpus Christi.

### Included

- Current storage, capacity, percent full, and surface area
- Daily, seven-day, and month-to-date evaporation
- 24-hour inflow, diversion, release, outflow, and storage-change accounting
- Estimated event-gain ranges and projected post-event storage
- Confidence, calculation basis, and mandatory changing-data caveat
- Reservoir-level readiness status and reasons
- JSON, CSV, readiness, and receipt outputs

Build057 remains complete, published, verified, and archived.
