## NRHIS Sprint 2 — Build053

Build053 adds official NOAA/NWS National Water Prediction Service forecasts and flood-threshold metadata to the NRHIS harvest engine.

### Included

- Gauge metadata and stage-flow endpoint retrieval
- Official forecast normalization
- Action, minor, moderate, and major threshold normalization
- Forecast peak timing and category classification
- Raw-response SHA-256 evidence
- Forecast, threshold, station-status, readiness, and receipt products

USGS remains the authoritative source for current observed river and water-quality values.
