## NRHIS Sprint 2 — Build070

Build070 begins the operator-facing deliverable phase by publishing the verified daily Nueces–Frio operations report in JSON, Markdown, and print-ready HTML.

### Included
- Four primary USGS station conditions
- Separate Choke Canyon and Lake Corpus Christi summaries
- Evaporation in acre-feet/day and MGD
- Seven-day and month-to-date evaporation totals
- 24-hour reservoir water budgets
- Estimated reservoir response
- SALT03 coastal water-quality status
- Scheduler health and alert status
- Two-pass QA publication hold
- Append-only report history and completion receipt
