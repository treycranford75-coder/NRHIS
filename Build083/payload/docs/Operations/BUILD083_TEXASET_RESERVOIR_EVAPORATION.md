# Build083 — TexasET-Informed Reservoir Evaporation

Build083 connects the Coastal Bend and Winter Garden TexasET regional summaries to the NRHIS reservoir-operations layer for Lake Corpus Christi and Choke Canyon Reservoir.

The calculation converts verified grass-reference ETo into a clearly labeled open-water evaporation range using configurable coefficients and the latest verified reservoir surface area. Outputs include inches per day, acre-feet per day, and million gallons per day.

TexasET ETo remains a proxy, not a direct reservoir-evaporation measurement. Reservoir-specific observations or validated energy-budget estimates take precedence whenever available.
