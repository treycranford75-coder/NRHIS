# Build082 - TexasET Regional ET

Adds a Texas A&M AgriLife Extension TexasET regional data channel for the Coastal Bend and Winter Garden areas.

Operational output includes verified station-level ETo and rainfall, regional mean ETo, station coverage, timestamps, and source URLs. ETo is grass-reference evapotranspiration and must not be presented as direct open-water reservoir evaporation. The data can support NRHIS agricultural-demand context, drought interpretation, and evaporation cross-checking.
