# Build085 Reservoir Water Budgets

Build085 produces a separate 24-hour water budget for Lake Corpus Christi and Choke Canyon Reservoir. Each budget reports beginning storage, inflow, municipal diversions, environmental releases, other releases, evaporation, net storage change, projected ending storage, and an optional observed-storage residual.

Evaporation values preserve the Build083-Build084 TexasET proxy caveat and may be replaced by verified direct open-water measurements when available.
