# Build084 — Reservoir Evaporation Rollups

Build084 adds reservoir-specific daily, trailing 7-day, and month-to-date evaporation losses to the NRHIS daily operations reporting layer. Lake Corpus Christi and Choke Canyon Reservoir remain separate throughout calculation and publication.

Each published value preserves the source, method, confidence, and estimate caveat. TexasET grass-reference ETo remains identified as a proxy rather than a direct open-water evaporation measurement.
