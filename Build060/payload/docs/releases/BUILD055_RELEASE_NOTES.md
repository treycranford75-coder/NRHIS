## NRHIS Sprint 2 — Build055

Build055 adds operational reservoir harvesting for Choke Canyon Reservoir and Lake Corpus Christi using TWDB Water Data for Texas.

### Products

- Current reservoir JSON and CSV
- Combined system storage summary
- Duplicate-safe reservoir history
- Raw GeoJSON evidence with SHA-256 receipt
- Stale-reservoir detection

### Source policy

TWDB Water Data for Texas is used for reservoir storage, capacity, percent full, elevation, volume, and surface area. Values are preserved with source dates and are not substituted with modeled estimates.
